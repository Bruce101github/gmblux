## SEO Strategy

### On-Page SEO Elements

1. **Title Tags**: 
   - Descriptive, keyword-rich titles
   - Include brand name and location
   - 50-60 characters optimal length

2. **Meta Descriptions**:
   - Compelling, informative descriptions
   - Include primary keywords
   - 150-160 characters optimal length

3. **Keywords**:
   - Location-based (Ghana, Accra)
   - Service-based (real estate, property brokerage)
   - Intent-based (property for sale, houses for rent)
   - Long-tail keywords for specific pages

4. **Structured Content**:
   - Clear heading hierarchy (H1, H2, H3)
   - Bullet points and lists for readability
   - Internal linking between pages
   - Call-to-action buttons

5. **Schema Markup**:
   - Using SEOHead component for meta tags
   - Open Graph tags for social sharing
   - Twitter Card tags
